How to Use the Extenstion

Installation

Press HERE and add the extension to chrome and agree with adding it to chrome.

Optional: To pin the extension locate the puzzle peice icon in the top of chrome and find "Website Speedruns".

Press the little 'pin' icon next to the extension.

How Open The Side Panel

Find the extension, right click, and locate to the "Open side panel" button.

How to Use

The 'Going from' and 'Going to' explain the objective of the game of trying to get from the first website to another.

The big timer is what shows you time for the run.

The buttons on at the bottom are the contols. This consists the 'Generate Websites' button, and the 'Start Run' button.

Generate Websites Button: This generates random websites from a list on where to go from and to.

Turning Off The Extension

Once you are done speedrunning, you will need to disable the extension. To do this click on the extension and locate to the

'Disable Extension' button. This will take you to the chrome extensions settings page. In here, you flip the swich

on the Website Speedruns panel.

If you want to play again, return to chrome://settings and turn the disabled switch back on and Website Speedruns will work as intended.
